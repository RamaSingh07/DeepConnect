Events

Explore events focused on liver health, awareness campaigns, and global initiatives that aim to educate, prevent, and manage liver-related conditions.

World Hepatitis Day(Important)

World Hepatitis Day is an annual event observed on July 28th to raise awareness about hepatitis, its prevention, and the global impact of the disease. It aims to educate the public and encourage governments to take action against this silent epidemic.

Read More

National Liver Health Campaign

The National Liver Health Campaign focuses on promoting healthy lifestyle choices to prevent liver diseases. The event features workshops, free screenings, and expert talks on the importance of early diagnosis and care for liver conditions.

Learn More

Liver Cancer Awareness Month

Held every October, Liver Cancer Awareness Month spreads knowledge about the signs, symptoms, and treatment options for liver cancer. The event highlights advancements in research and supports those living with liver cancer and helps them .

Explore More

Global Liver Health Summit

The Global Liver Health Summit gathers healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers to discuss innovations and strategies in liver disease prevention and management. It is a platform for collaboration and sharing groundbreaking research.
