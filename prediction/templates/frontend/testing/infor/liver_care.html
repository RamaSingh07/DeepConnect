Liver Diagnosis and Care

Early diagnosis and proper care are crucial in managing liver conditions effectively. Learn more about the key stages of diagnosing and caring for your liver health.

Blood Tests for Liver Health

Blood tests such as liver function tests (LFTs) are the first step in diagnosing liver problems. They measure enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin levels to assess liver health and detect abnormalities.

Read More

Imaging Tests and Liver Biops

Imaging techniques like ultrasounds, CT scans, and MRIs provide detailed views of the liver. In some cases, a liver biopsy is needed to collect tissue samples for further diagnosis.

Learn More

Managing Chronic Liver Diseases

Chronic liver conditions require ongoing care. Lifestyle changes, medications, and regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider can help manage symptoms and prevent complications.

Explore More

Importance of Early Screening

Early screening can help detect liver diseases in their initial stages, improving treatment outcomes. Regular health check-ups and awareness are essential to maintaining liver health.
