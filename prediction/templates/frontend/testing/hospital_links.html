Find India's Best Hospitals for Liver Conditions

India has many top hospitals for liver disease, including hospitals that specialize in liver transplants and hospitals that offer comprehensive liver care.

Apollo Hospitals

A global leader in liver transplants with a 90% success rate. They offer advanced surgical methods and have a dedicated Hepatobiliary Critical Care Unit. They are unique and state of the art facilities spread across several of the Apollo hospital locations and each Centre of Excellence stands out as a citadel of world class clinical outcomes.

Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital

A hospital in Mumbai that's known for its success in liver transplants. Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, is ranked amongst the top hospitals in India and has been awarded the number 1 hospital in Western India

Sir Ganga Ram Hospital

A hospital in New Delhi that offers both living-donor and cadaveric transplants.The Sir Ganga Ram Hospital also provides training to young doctors under the Diplomate in National Board (DNB) program.

Artemis Hospital

A super specialty hospital in Gurgaon that offers comprehensive liver care. They have advanced technologies like 64 Slice Cardiac CT Scans.Artemis Hospital is a JCI and NABH accredited hospital in Gurgaon, India.

Medanta Hospital

Global Health Limited, Medanta, is an Indian for-profit private hospital network based in Gurgaon. A multi-specialty hospital that has performed more than 600 liver transplants.

Breach Candy Hospital

A hospital in Mumbai that offers personalized treatment plans and has a high success rate in liver transplants. It is a well-known and respected hospital that offers a wide range of medical services in various specialities.

Dr. Rela's Institute & Medical Centre

Rela Hospital is a leading private multispecialty hospital located in Chromepet, Chennai, known for delivering world-class healthcare with a patient-centric approach.
